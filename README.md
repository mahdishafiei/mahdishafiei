## About Me

I'm a **PhD candidate in Bioinformatics & Computational Biology** at [Scripps Research](https://www.scripps.edu/) in the [Briney Lab](https://github.com/brineylab), based in San Diego, CA.

I develop computational methods at the intersection of **machine learning**, **immunology**, and **genomics** to understand the adaptive immune system. My work spans antibody language model scaling, protein structure analysis, and biostatistical modeling.

I'm passionate about turning complex biological data into actionable insights using modern ML/AI approaches and rigorous statistical methods.

---

## Research Interests

- **Antibody / Immune Repertoire Analysis** — scaling laws for antibody language models
- **Structural Biology & Protein Engineering** — sequence-structure relationships
- **Biostatistics & Epidemiology** — clustering, latent class analysis, survey-based research
- **Deep Learning for Biology** — semantic segmentation, representation learning
